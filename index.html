about me

hi i'm Noor Hanafi welcome to this about me page!

As a student, I find myself always curious about the things I learn every day from information that I learn from my mom when we have our daily talk about the world we live in while sitting in the bedroom to reading scientific articles about recent discoveries in our world that is full of secrets. I love consuming art, and here I am not just talking about literature and drawing, though I adore them too, but I'm talking about art that is planted in our world from chemistry and its roots that are in everything and every concept in front of our functioning eyes to physics and mathematics that are the base for every shape and function in front of us with their laws limiting and unlimiting every movement we make and every invention we think of; and honestly, I don't think I'm the only one thinking that way as my way of thinking was the same with every artist and scientist around our world and the thing that makes our discoveries and inventions more beautiful and interesting is how cultures surround it to make the final piece more beautiful than what it was already. Finally, as a final statement, I would like to say that as I said before, I adore art, and for me, learning itself is art, and that very well describes the student I am

I'm a sucker for learning new things but i always stick to my roots which is Computer Science and now most of the time i use my knowledge in new fun projects that i work on and i am also still learning new things! but this was not possible years ago because i did not know what to do in the paste so now i made this website so you can have the things i found easily so you can go learn with ease!
